Semantic Tags(header, main & footer)

Header Tag(1st semantic tag)

Contain all the info that needs to be displayed in the header of the webpage. 

            
Portfolio                  About    |     Education    |    Experience

Main Tag(2nd semantic tag)

Section Tag

Education : Mphil | PhD
Experience : 1 year Microsoft | MS intern

Section tag divides all the parts into different sections to enhance readability.

Article Tag

This is the main content of the page. It talks about a primary topic in detail.

Like; I am a coder currently learning & practicing CSS.

Related Info( Aside Tag)

This is some additional information related to the main content, like links, ads, or author bio.

Google does read

content, but treats it as less important than main content for SEO.

Exp:- About me: I am a beginner coder currently learning, practicing experimenting with HTML.
